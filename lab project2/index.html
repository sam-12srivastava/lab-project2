Legendary Anand Kumar

Struggles

  • Faced financial difficulties in early life.
  • Could not afford to study abroad due to family constraints.
  • Started Super 30 to help underprivileged students.
  • Overcame societal challenges to pursue his passion for teaching.

Achievements

  • Founded Super 30 program in 2002.
  • Helped hundreds of students crack IIT entrance exams.
  • Featured in various national and international media.
  • Received numerous awards and recognitions for his work.

Impact

  • Changed lives of many underprivileged students.
  • Inspired educational reforms in India.
  • Highlighted the importance of accessible education.
  • Continues to motivate aspiring mathematicians and educators.
